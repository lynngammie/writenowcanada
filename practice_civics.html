Write now, Canada!

A simple and intelligent way to draft a letter to your local councillor, MPP or MP about the government issues that matter to you. Choose a municipal, provincial or federal issue below and get writing.

The municipal government makes decisions on by-laws concerning public transportation, municipal parks, social services and more.

The provincial government deals with issues that affect the province, such as health care, education and the environment.

The federal government is responsible for issues that affect Canada as a whole, such as immigration, foreign policy and international trade.
